Visualization Feedback

Please provide feedback for the individual views and features of the visualization. Each view and feature is briefly presented and explained. If you had not previously explored or used the feature, you may specify so.

Map and List of Places

The map (left) and list of places (right) show the geographical context of religious groups. They let you see which religious groups existed in which cities, and allow to filter and search for place names.

How important and helpful do you consider the map and list of places to be?
How much time did you approximately spend using the map and list of places?

Timeline

The timeline shows the presence of religious groups at different times. Each religious group is represented as a row, which is colored if we have evidence of that religious group at that time. The timeline can also be used to specify a filter criterion for data shown, by selecting a rectangular area.

How important and helpful do you consider the timeline to be?
How much time did you approximately spend using the timeline?

Religion hierarchy

The religion hierarchy shows the different religious groups. The groups are shown hierarchically under their respective main religion. Checkboxes behind each religious group can be used to filter the dataset for specific religious groups.

How important and helpful do you consider the religion hierarchy to be?
How much time did you approximately spend using the religion hierarchy?
